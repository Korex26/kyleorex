about me

Hi, I am Kyle Owen Rex. I am a dedicated college scholar committed to the highest levels of service to the United States of America's Strategic Engineering Defense Industry. I am on track to graduate from Texas A&M University in May of 2026 with a Bachelor of Science in Mechatronics Engineering and a minor in Embedded Systems Integration. I am a member of the Texas A&M Corps of Cadets and will commission into the US Army Reserves upon graduation where I will serve 1 weekend a month and 2 weeks during the summer while pursuing my fulltime long-term engineering career.

In my early career I am hoping to focus on learning and developing myself into a qualified technical engineer capable of understanding the complexities of different engineering focused roles and responsibilities. I plan to execute these roles to the highest standard of excellence and ultimately have the privilege of becoming a more experienced engineer. In this, hopefully lifelong, career I plan to compete for positions of increasing complexity, responsibility, and leadership with aspirations to ultimately be qualified for a management position. I believe beyond my engineering skills I have a lot to offer to in organizational management, servant leadership, and strategic project planning.
My favorite type of work is, and always will be, helping others. Whenever I am able to help a colleague, friend, or fellow student it is the most rewarding feeling ever. Passionately helping others accomplish their goals and tasks is what ultimately drives my desire to pursue a project lead position. I believe in this type of position I can make the most impact positively influencing my collogues work and my team's objectives in support of the corporation's overall missions, goals, and success.
I believe I will be the happiest doing collaborative work having the opportunity to rely on the skills and experience of those around me to the advantage of the project. I understand the importance of creating a network that you can rely on. Strategically assigning tasks, based on my understanding of the abilities of my fellow colleagues, is instrumental to the overall success of the project. Given the opportunity I look forward to being the supportive, understanding, and flexible leader that is required in today's corporate America to aid in the overall success of the team, organization, and corporation.
Thank you for taking the time to read about me. I hope you gained insight on my personal career and professional goals. I look forward to connecting in the future. Thanks!
